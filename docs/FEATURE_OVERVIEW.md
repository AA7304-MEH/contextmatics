# ContextMatic — Feature Overview

## Email & Social Authentication
Users can seamlessly sign up using email links, Google, GitHub, or Azure OAuth providers. This reduces friction during onboarding and ensures higher conversion rates for new users.

## Multi-Platform Content Generation
Creators can generate tailored posts for platforms like Twitter and LinkedIn from identical base prompts. It deeply integrates platform-specific constraints like character limits and thread styles to maximize engagement.

## URL Content Repurposing
Users can paste a single URL and instantly receive six distinct platform-optimized social media posts. This unique capability compresses hours of research and writing into seconds of automated summarization.

## Brand Voice Studio
The voice studio analyzes uploaded text samples to generate a unique linguistic fingerprint. This ensures all future AI-generated content sounds authentically human and identical to the creator's natural tone.

## Faceless Video Studio
The platform converts text prompts into fully produced short-form videos with background footage, captions, and AI voiceovers. It allows creators to build highly engaging TikToks or Reels without ever stepping in front of a camera.

## Automated Post Scheduling
Users can arrange their generated content onto a unified calendar that connects directly to their social accounts via Ayrshare. This removes the need for expensive third-party scheduling tools and keeps the entire workflow centralized.

## Audience Inbox
The platform aggregates comments and replies across multiple social platforms into a single interactive dashboard. By streamlining engagement, creators can build strongest community relationships without platform-hopping.

## Competitor Tracking
Users can silently monitor competitor behavior and extract high-performing post strategies directly into their workspace. This structural advantage helps creators reverse-engineer viral frameworks and adapt them for their own niche.

## Lead Magnet Monetisation
Creators can automatically generate valuable PDF lead magnets to capture email subscribers directly from their social profiles. This turns passive followers into owned audience members, creating a direct path to monetization.

## Credit-Based Billing
The system tracks generative API usage accurately and charges users through secure Razorpay and PayPal integrations. This granular control allows for scalable profit margins while users pay exactly for what they consume.

## Admin Dashboard
Administrators can oversee platform health, grant manual credit balances, and monitor user activity in real-time. This provides complete operational control and helps quickly troubleshoot individual user issues.
